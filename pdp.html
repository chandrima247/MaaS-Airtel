Waiting Periods

Be aware of these timelines before using certain benefits.

General Illness

30 days from policy start.

Pre-existing & Chronic Conditions

1 year from policy start.

Cancer Treatment

2 years from policy start.

Congenital & Inherited Conditions

1 year from policy start.

Waiting periods begin from the policy effective date. After they are satisfied, covered conditions become eligible for benefits subject to the plan terms.
